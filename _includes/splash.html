Austin Lawrence

Austin Lawrence is a recent graduate with a Master of Science in Robotics student at Northwestern University.He is attracted towards problems that have the biggest global impact, especially in topics of sustainability and urban congestion. In 2012, he co-founded an ag-tech startup, Future Tech Farm, and has had his work featured in the New York Times, NPR, and Seedstock. Presently, Austin is seeking to use his robotics study to reinvent modern agriculture in a paradigm shift of scalable autonomous technologies. Additionally, he is assuming an active research role in fields of computer vision, machine learning, dynamics, mechatronics, and manipulation.
